More About Me

I'm a passionate and driven graphic designer with over 2 years of experience helping startups, apparel brands, and e-commerce businesses create impactful visual identities. My goal is to combine creativity, strategy, and design thinking to deliver visuals that not only look good but also drive real business results.

My journey began with a deep interest in branding and a desire to bring ideas to life through visuals. Being self-taught through free online resources, I’ve developed a strong foundation in logo design, brand identity, social media content, and marketing collateral — with a growing skill in basic motion and logo animation.

I’ve had the chance to work on projects for emerging apparel brands like Aurelia and Veq, as well as a watch company named Zovet, building identities that reflect their vision and connect with their audience.

When I'm not designing, I'm exploring design trends, learning new tools, or experimenting with creative side projects like car posters and packaging ideas. I'm always curious, always learning — and always excited to work with brands that want to stand out.
